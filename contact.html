How to contact TbdEducation

You can find answers to common queries, getting started guides, troubleshooting tips and more in our Help Centre.

If you wish to get in touch with us, there are a number of ways to do so.

Support

If you need help using TbdEducation, just press the ‘support’ button at the bottom right of any course page.

Alternatively you can submit a request directly.

If you are having technical problems with TbdEducation, please check our Status Blog to see if it is an issue we are currently fixing.

If you would like to make a complaint, please see our complaints procedure.

Media and Press Enquiries

Please send an email to media.enquiries@TbdEducation.com. You can find more information in our Press area.

Becoming a Partner

Interested in becoming a TbdEducation Partner?

Email us at partner.enquiries@TbdEducation.com.

Your stories

Have a great story about your experience on TbdEducation? Send us an e-mail at yourstories@TbdEducation.com. We’d love to hear from you.

You can also follow us on Twitter and Facebook.
